Live Life

On purpose and do what you love

Hi, my name is Kim Fuller

My mission is to ignite passion for your life by identifying what stops and moves you forward and reflecting it back to you in a way that allows you to live life on purpose.

I am so excited to work with you, using the science of hand analysis to identify your Life Purpose, Life Lesson and Life School, your soul psychology, then filling in the rest of your story using the lines & shapes of your hands - the personality psychology. Thank you for entrusting me with your story.

I live in the beautiful state of Vermont which has so many beautiful things to see, expansive views to minute things my eyes are drawn to during adventures in the woods. Using these talents, I identify both the big picture and the minute details of your life through the lens of your hands and the information they hold.
